IMPOSTER

HOW TO
PLAY
The Setup

Everyone taps their name to privately see their role. One or more players are secretly the Imposter — they get a cryptic hint instead of the real word.

The Three Hint Modes
🏷️ Category — Imposter only knows the category. Hardest mode.
💬 Word Hint — Imposter gets the category + one sneaky word hint.
🎭 Charades — No talking! Everyone must act out or make noises only.
Playing the Game

Go around the table. Each player gives one clue related to the word. Then everyone votes to eliminate who they think is the Imposter.

Winning

Crewmates win by eliminating all Imposters.
Imposters win by surviving the vote — or by correctly guessing the word after being caught.
