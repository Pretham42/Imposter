HOW TO PLAY
The Setup

Everyone gets a word. One or more players are secretly the Imposter — they get a cryptic hint instead of the real word.

The Hints
Category — The imposter only knows the broad category (e.g. "NBA").
Normal — The imposter gets one sneaky word hint.
Charades — No words allowed! Everyone acts/makes noises only.
How to Win

Crewmates take turns giving one clue. Try to spot who doesn't actually know the word. Vote to eliminate the Imposter!

Imposters blend in by giving convincing clues. If you survive the vote, you win!
